# **App Name**: FCT Youth Connect

## Core Features:

- Organization Directory: List registered youth organizations with contact details and a short description.
- Youth Parliament Information: Provide information on the FCT-Abuja Youth Parliament, including its members, committees, and how to join.
- Opportunities Aggregator: Aggregate scholarship, internship, job, and grant opportunities for youth.
- Automated Content Generation for Opportunities: Use AI to generate descriptions, potential eligibility, and short summaries of listed opportunities for greater engagement with available opportunities. A tool to help surface appropriate info for opportunities.
- Media Center: Showcase photos and videos from recent events and outreach programs.
- Contact Form: A form allowing users to quickly contact the FCT for inquiries, suggestions, and feedback.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Emerald green (#32CD32) to represent growth, opportunity, and the FCT's commitment to youth.
- Background color: Very light green (#F0FFF0), nearly white, to maintain a clean and accessible feel.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) for interactive elements, links, and buttons to create a vibrant interface that emphasizes Calls To Action.
- Headline Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, for titles and headings throughout the website.
- Body Font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for the site body to ensure clear, accessible information presentation.
- Use outline-style icons to represent categories such as education, jobs, and events; make sure to pick icons that reinforce the idea of 'opportunity' and 'connection'.
- Subtle transition effects for page navigation, modal windows opening, and any Call To Action. Aim for smooth, gentle effects.
